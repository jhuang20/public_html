WHAT DO WE DO?

A Program Co-Operated With The SU

Stuyvesant Student Union

CitiBike X Stuy would like to thank the Stuyvesant Student Union for coordinating the school-side logistics of this program. This program is sponsored by CitiBike and the Stuyvesant Student Union, and is part of a larger initiative by the current SU Administration to improve student quality of life. We are glad to have them as partners!
