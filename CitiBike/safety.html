Safety

Our Number One Priority is Safety

Whether You're a first time rider, or an experienced cyclist, be sure to follow the rules of the road

Road Safety


-Wear a Helmet


-yield to pedestrians
-always be aware of your surrounding
-follow all marked signs, use the bike lane
-stop at red lights
-don't be afraid to take the whole lane
-watch for wide right turns from buses

General Safety


-be aware of your surrounding
-cycle with a friend
-don't exhaust yourself, bring water
-NEVER text and bike
-Use google maps to find the safest route
-dress adequqtely for the weather
-don't ride outside your comfort zone
