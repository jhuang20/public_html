Why Do I Have To Take A Safety Class?


Hey There!

Right now, you may be wondering why you have to sign up for a safety class before recieving your free CitiBike membership. The answer lies in student safety and liability. As a public high school in NYC, Stuyvesant is responsible for its students' safety to a large extent. Thus, to get administrative approval, it was necessary to include some safety measures to lower the (already low) chance of a student injuring themselves.

Stuyvesant students have, in the past, been victims of reckless driving. While, unfortunately, new York has and will have a disproportionately large share of reckless drivers in the USA, there are many steps you can take to prevent yourself from being a victim of bad judgement. This Will be discussed in depth in the 3 hour class.

Additionally, CitiBike's system may be new to you guys, and within the 3 hour class, you will be able to practice undocking, docking and riding a citibike safely. The class may be 3 hours long-- but it will definitely feel shorter! As a token of thanks for taking this safety class, you Will recieve a helmet, some swag, and coming soon, clip-on headlights. It's just our token of thanks for taking your time to learn about being safe.

Oh, and of course, a free annual CitiBike membership! This is better than student Metrocards-it's valid at 4:30 on a Monday and 3am on a Sunday(not That I would ever condone being out at 3AM on a sunday).
